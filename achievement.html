EDUCATION
Quality education is the main focus of Rumah Aman for the children. There is a Malay saying "Membentuk aur biarlah dari rebung" which can be literally translated as "To bend a bamboo, begin from the shoot". This is the basis why Rumah Aman enrolled children from the age of three to six years old into the orphanage and welfare home. Rumah Aman's education programme is a continuous learning of life skills based on the Quran and the Sunna of Prophet Muhammad sallallahu 'alaihi wasallam (May Allah bless him and grant him peace) and integrated with the knowledge of the time. This continuous learning is for all who are with Rumah Aman whether permanent staffs or volunteers because they will be the role models for the children to follow as the maxim goes "children copy adults". This pattern of learning is paramount because only then the children will have a strong foundation in the Deen of Islam as well as a basis for them to be victorious in this world and in the Hereafter. The education programme of Rumah Aman is called I-SMART.
I-SMART is an acronym for :
IMAN - As stated in the Pillars of Iman, is the believe in Allah, in the Prophets, in the Angels, in the Kitabs (Books), in Qada and Qadar and in the Day of Judgement. It also teaches the Muslims about the ghaib (unseen world) and of taqwa.
SOLAT – The pillar of Ibadah and the reference for good adab. Making wudhu is a practice of being clean always and a reminder of preferred order. With solat comes the remembrance of Allah subhanahu wa ta'ala the discipline of keeping time and the importance to lead and to follow with correct knowledge.
MUHASABAH – Is the practice of correcting oneself continuously and consistently as man is not perfect by taking on the behaviour of the Rasul sallallahu 'alaihi wa sallam (May Allah bless him and grant him peace) and guidance from the Quran.
ACCOUNTABILITY – Is the preferred right actions with Allah subhanahu wa ta'ala and with mankind. All actions either right or wrong have a cause and effect that will be attributed to the doer; therefore the doer must have a clear mind and clear understanding of his/her intentions and actions.
RESPONSIBILITY – Is the awareness that man is the Khalifah of Allah. Man is using borrowed attributes of Allah subhanahu wa ta'ala therefore all decisions and actions which impinge on the lives of all living creatures must be for the pleasure of Allah subhanahu wa ta'ala and within His guided parameters.
TAQWA – Awe or fear of Allah which inspires a person to be on guard against wrong actions and eager for actions which pleases Him.
